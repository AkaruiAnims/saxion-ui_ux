CMGT ROCKS JUN 7, 2024

Posted on: Tuesday, 4 June 2024 13:39:02 o'clock CEST


Hey all,


this Friday, the 7th of June, we will host the 2nd CMGT ROCKS event to celebrate the musical culture at CMGT!

The goal is to provide students (and teachers) who have a passion for music a stage to rock their lungs out! And you can support them!

We have 4 bands joining - Veridian Hollow (featuring students Veerle and Lucas), Spumoni (feat. Lisanne), Orion (feat. Jasper) and MOTBAK (featuring Mark B and Paul B).


So bring your band shirt and air guitar and rock on this Friday!




Location: The Jolly Jug (Oude Markt, next to Molly Malone)

Date: Fri 7th of June

Start: 19:00
