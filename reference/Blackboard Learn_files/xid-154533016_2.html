Minor enrolment @Saxion opens tonight!

Posted on: Monday, 11 March 2024 11:59:55 o'clock CET

This message is intended for year 2 students.

 

Will you be taking a minor during the fall semester (term 1 and 2, September 2024-January 2025)? Now is the time to discover all possibilities and find out more about our offer. More information about our offer is available via mysaxion.nl/minors or saxion.nl/minoren. Which minor do you choose? A minor is a compulsory one-semester elective course worth 30 credits (ECTS). You take a minor after passing your first year (propaedeutic) examination, usually in the third academic year of your Bachelor's programme.

 

What is a minor?

A minor is a compulsory six-month elective course worth 30 credits (ECTS). You take a minor after passing your first year examination, usually in the third or fourth academic year of your Bachelor programme. 

 

More information about our minors offer is available online

Go to mysaxion.nl/minors and saxion.nl/minoren to discover Saxion‚Äôs complete minors offer. Here you will find information about all Saxion minors, including details of contact persons.

 

Prepare yourself thoroughly

Alongside the complete minors offer, mysaxion.nl/minors contains extensive guidance about types of minors, planning and procedures. You will also find information about taking a minor at another university of applied sciences or transferring to the University of Twente for a follow-up study. This information can help you familiarise yourself with the offer and make your decision.

 

Apply from Monday 11 March

You can apply for minor programmes starting in September 2024 through Bison or mysaxion.nl/minors from 19:00 hours on Monday 11 March 2024. The enrolment window for minor programmes is open until 24 May 2024.

 

Minors offered by CMGT

For Minors offered by Saxion (CMGT and other programmes), you can visit the General information about the minor. The most frequently chosen minors by CMGT students are:

Skilled (CMGT only!)

The minor Skilled allows you to work on a specific skillset. It encourages you to take a systematic approach to research the workflow, tools, and knowledge required for a particular skillset and adopt a professional attitude to your work. The result is outstanding content for your portfolio and an increased level of self-discipline.

Business & Data Analytics (Saxion meet Silicon Valley!)

This minor is a cooperation between Saxion and the UC Berkeley. The minor includes a studytrip to the US, to the University of California, Berkeley. During the minor you get the unique opportunity to collect and apply state of the art knowledge and skills about a diversity of topics. During the minor, you visit UC Berkeley and Silicon Valley for three weeks (November 2024). You learn all about the 'power of Silicon Valley' and attend masterclasses and seminars in: Business and Data Analytics, Machine Learning, AI, CryptoAsset Tracking, Data Mining and Design Thinking. Also you will work with a projectgroup at an actual assignment for a company.

How to Create Killer Content

The exchange programme ‚ÄòHow to create Killer Content‚Äù will teach you how to create a story, translate it into a concept which engages your target group and establishes long lasting relationships. But how to create a concept for a company which will have great impact on their target group? You will need to dive deep into their world. Both, company and target group. Combine this with your creativity, your look at the world and you will find new angles for a concept!

Liberal Arts & Sciences (in Deventer)

During the minor students will develop into curious, conscious, multidisciplinary international professionals who are able to look at the world from various perspectives and make connections between different disciplines and philosophies. A wide range of subjects like physiology, geopolitics, societal issues, earth sciences and culture will be discussed. After attending the minor the students are able ‚Äúto connect the dots‚Äù and will therefore act more as convincing and trustworthy actors. They will be able to relate with to any counterpart they face in their private or business network and interact with them in a sensible and eloquent way.
 

Immersive 4D Design (in Deventer, taught in Dutch!)

Stel je voor dat je de wereld om je heen kunt veranderen in een meeslepende ervaring. Tijdens de minor Immersive 4D Design krijg je de kans om te experimenteren met innovatieve oplossingen voor ruimtelijke projecten. Met tools zoals virtual reality (VR) en extended reality (XR), projecties, geur- en geluidsontwerp heb je als toekomstig ontwerper krachtige middelen om baanbrekende en duurzame ontwerpen te maken, waarmee je het gewenste verhaal en de gewenste ervaring kunt overbrengen.

Want to know more? Check Saxion.nl, My Saxion, or, make an appointment with me.
You can book an individual or group meeting via MS bookings.

Good luck with choosing an inspiring minor!

Kind regards,
Johannes de Boer
Minor coordinator CMGT

Do you want to take a minor during the spring semester (February-July 2023)?  Now is the time to discover all possibilities and learn more about our offer!

 

A minor is a compulsory one semester worth 30 credits (ECTS). You take a minor after passing your first year (propaedeutic) examination, usually in the third academic year of your Bachelor programme.
